National

During the recession of 2007, the number of workers with some college or Associate's degrees fell by almost two million but has regained all of its losses since then.

However, there have been even larger gains in the number workers with Bachelor's degrees or higher.

Since the recession, the economy has shed workers without any college experience. These losses have stabilized since 2010.
